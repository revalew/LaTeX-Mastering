\section{Optional Arguments in the Documentclass}

We have the:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item 12pt - to change the default font size from 10 to 12 pt
    \item fleqn - change the equation display - float left
    \item leqno - change the number of the equation - float left
    \item twocolumn - the document will have two horizontal columns
    \item landscape - self explanatory
\end{enumerate}

\section{The Letter Documentclass}

To create the letter we can do something like this:
\begin{verbatim}
    \documentclass[12pt]{letter}

    \signature{Me}
    \address{Funkytown \\ Funkystreet \\ Funky house}

    \begin{document}
        \begin{letter}{CEO \\ Business INC \\ Business address}
            % can take 2nd argument like the name of the company
            \opening{To whom it may concern:}

            I hope You are doing well.

            \closing{Yours Faithfully.}
        \end{letter}
    \end{document}
\end{verbatim}
